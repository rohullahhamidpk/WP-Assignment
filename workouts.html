Workout Routines

Full Body Workout

Description: A comprehensive workout that targets all major muscle groups.

Exercises:

  • Squats: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Bench Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Deadlifts: 3 sets of 8 reps
  • Pull-Ups: 3 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Tips: Ensure proper form to avoid injuries and maximize efficiency.

Cardio Routine

Description: A high-energy routine focused on cardiovascular fitness.

Exercises:

  • Running: 20 minutes at moderate pace
  • Jump Rope: 3 sets of 2 minutes
  • Burpees: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 15 reps

Tips: Keep a steady pace and focus on your breathing.

Strength Training

Description: Targeted exercises to build muscle strength.

Exercises:

  • Deadlifts: 4 sets of 6 reps
  • Bench Press: 4 sets of 6 reps
  • Barbell Rows: 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Overhead Press: 4 sets of 6 reps

Tips: Gradually increase weights for best results.
